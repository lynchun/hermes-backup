gut, sinuses, eyes, and skin — a fungal-driven inflammatory syndrome, not
primarily MCAS or dysautonomia.

## Protocol Summary

### Supplements Daily
- Boron 3mg twice daily
- Methyl folate 1000mcg twice daily
- Methyl B12 ~1000mcg twice daily
- Broad-spectrum probiotic (≥10B CFU) daily
- Saccharomyces boulardii daily (alternate with bacterial)
- Vitamin C 500mg twice daily
- Lactoferrin 250mg twice daily
- Caprylic acid 500-1000mg 2-3x daily
- Berberine 500mg twice daily (⚠️ drug interactions)
- Aged garlic extract ~1200mg daily (⚠️ not with blood thinners)

### Medicines
- Ivermectin 20mg weekly x 4 weeks
- Topical ivermectin 0.1% / metronidazole 1% gel — eyelids at bedtime

### Sinus Rinse (daily x 28 days)
- 0.25-0.5% betadine solution (6-12ml of 10% in 240ml irrigation bottle)
- +4ml Johnson Baby Shampoo (hypoallergenic tear-free)
- Head forward 45-90°, irrigate until exits other nostril
- Lie flat 5-10 min post-rinse, then lean forward and gently blow nose
- ⚠️ Stains clothing
- Weekly probiotic sinus rinse (start ¼ capsule due to die-off risk)

### Body Wash
- Microshield Povidone Iodine Surgical Handwash (shampoo + body wash, leave on several minutes)

### Eye Care
- Tea tree oil lid wipes daily (prevents Demodex recolonisation)
