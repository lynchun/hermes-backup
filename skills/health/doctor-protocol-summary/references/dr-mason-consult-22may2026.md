## Treatment Protocol

### Supplements Daily

1. Boron 3mg twice daily
2. Methylated Folate (NOW, 1000mcg) twice daily
3. Methylated B12 (~1000mcg) twice daily
4. Broad-spectrum bacterial probiotic (≥10B CFU) daily — LifeSpace Broad Spectrum
5. Saccharomyces boulardii daily — alternate or alongside #4
6. Vitamin C 500mg twice daily
7. Lactoferrin 250mg twice daily or 500mg once daily
8. Caprylic acid 500-1000mg 2-3 times daily — NOW brand caprylic acid 600mg
9. Berberine 500mg twice daily — ⚠️ multiple drug interactions
10. Aged garlic extract / Kyolic garlic ~1200mg daily — not with blood thinners

### Medicines Weekly

1. Ivermectin 20mg weekly x 4 weeks
2. Topical ivermectin 0.1% / metronidazole 1% gel — eyelids at bedtime

### Sinus Rinse (28 days)

- Betadine 0.25-0.5% solution: 6-12ml of 10% betadine + 4ml Johnson Baby Shampoo in 240ml bottle
- Head forward 45-90°, irrigate until exits other nostril
- Lie flat 5-10 min post-rinse for dwell time
- Probiotic rinse weekly: start ¼ capsule, saline first
- ⚠️ Die-off reaction possible, stains clothing

### Body Wash

Microshield Povidone Iodine Surgical Handwash — leave on several minutes

### Eye Care (Ongoing)

- TTO lid wipes daily/every second day
- Topical ivermectin/metronidazole gel at bedtime
