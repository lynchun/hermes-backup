## Observed Patterns

### 4-Period Averages (24h clock)

| Period | Hours | May 2025 Avg | May 2026 Avg | Pattern |
|--------|-------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| Overnight | 00:00-05:59 | 0.33 | 0.41 | 2026 higher overnight |
| Morning | 06:00-11:59 | 0.38 | 0.29 | Both decline through morning |
| Afternoon | 12:00-17:59 | 0.43 | 0.32 | 2025 peaks mid-afternoon |
| Evening | 18:00-23:59 | 0.42 | 0.47 | Both rise through evening |

Circadian pattern: ketones rise through evening (fasting effect), lowest midday (fed state). Consistent across both sensor sessions.

### Key Findings (Updated May 2026)

1. **Sensor insertion spike:** Day 1 of each session shows markedly higher readings than the rest — likely from the sensor insertion/tissue trauma. The 2026 session peaked at 1.5 mmol/L on day 1 (first ~3 hours). Don't over-interpret day 1 data.

2. **Gradual decline:** Both sessions trend downward after the first 24-48h, then stabilise in the 0.2-0.4 range. This is normal for ketone CGM — tissue ketone levels drift down as the sensor site heals.

3. **Minimal time in nutritional ketosis (>0.5):** Only ~30-34% of readings exceed 0.5 mmol/L in either session. This is lower than expected for strict carnivore. Possible reasons: sensor accuracy at low levels, intermittent dietary excursions, or individual metabolic efficiency. Worth flagging for Dr Mason.

4. **Remarkable consistency between years:** Despite being 12 months apart, the two sensors show nearly identical profiles — avg 0.39 vs 0.38, median 0.30 vs 0.30, max 1.4 vs 1.5. The distribution is nearly identical year-over-year.

## Clinical Interpretation for Dr Mason

- Lyndon spends ~30% of time in nutritional ketosis (>0.5 mmol/L) based on CGM
- Minimal time at "therapeutic" levels (>1.0) — only 2-4% of readings
- The pattern suggests consistent but relatively low-level ketosis
- Session-over-session reproducibility implies this is a stable metabolic baseline, not an artifact
- Compare with serum/handheld ketone readings if available to cross-validate CGM accuracy
