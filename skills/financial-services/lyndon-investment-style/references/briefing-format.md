# Briefing Format Rules for Lyndon

These apply to daily market briefings and any market commentary delivered to Lyndon.

## Conciseness
- No fluff, no filler. Every line must earn its place.
- Remove anything Lyndon didn't ask for.

## Data
- WTI Crude only — never Brent. Yahoo Finance homepage shows Brent in the ticker bar; navigate to CL=F separately.
- No Dow 30 unless specifically requested.
- Watchlist symbols: S&P 500 (SPY/SPX), NASDAQ (NDX), VIX, Gold (XAUUSD), Silver (XAGUSD), WTI (CL=F), SILJ, GDX.

## No editorialising
- Never add commentary like "Getting thin. Consider topping up" or "Your positioning maps to this thesis."
- Report data, let Lyndon interpret.
- No investment advice.

## Omit these sections
- No "Watchlist — Articles Worth Opening" section.
- No label comments on tickers (e.g., "← Junior silver miners").
- No "Current Projects" commentary beyond factual status.

## Format
- Clean, scannable, terminal-friendly.
- Compact sections: one line per ticker.
- Themes at the end are fine if they summarise, not advise.
